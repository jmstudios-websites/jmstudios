FAQ

Questions before starting a website.

How long does a website usually take?

Most standard websites can be ready in about 1 week once the content and direction are clear.

Can you handle hosting and domain setup?

Yes. The Hosting Care plan can cover hosting management, domain connection, DNS records, SSL, and routine technical support.

Do I need to already have a domain?

No. If you already own one, it can be connected. If you do not, I can help you choose and set up the right domain.

What do I need to provide?

Usually your business name, page ideas, written content, images if you have them, and any examples of websites you like.

Can the website be updated later?

Yes. Small updates can be handled after launch, especially if you choose ongoing hosting or care support.

How fast do you respond?

I usually respond within 1-2 days with the best next step, estimate, or scope questions.
