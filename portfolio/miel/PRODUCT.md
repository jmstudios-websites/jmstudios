# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow developers landing on this portfolio. They're scanning quickly — deciding in 10 seconds whether to keep reading. Context: desktop browser, professional evaluation mode.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio site for a creative frontend developer. Exists to make a strong first impression and communicate craft, taste, and technical range through the design itself. Success = someone reaches out or saves the link.

## Brand Personality

Precise. Quiet. Lethal. The work speaks; the site doesn't shout.

## Anti-references

- Generic Bootstrap/template portfolios with carousel sliders
- Neon-heavy "creative coder" sites with too much motion and too little content
- Over-animated agency sites where you can't find the work
- Hero-metric SaaS clichés (big numbers, gradient text, glass cards)

## Design Principles

1. Show, don't tell — the design quality IS the portfolio
2. Restraint over decoration — every element earns its place
3. Typographic confidence — hierarchy through scale and weight, not color
4. Purposeful motion — one or two signature moments, nothing gratuitous
5. Scannable at speed — a recruiter can extract name, role, and two projects in 8 seconds

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. High contrast text throughout.
