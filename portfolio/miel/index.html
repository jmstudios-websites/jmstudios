Our story

Started at 4am. Still is.

Miel opened in 2019 with one oven, one baker, and a firm belief that good bread takes time. We never set out to grow quickly. We set out to bake something worth waking up for.

Today we're a small team of eight, still working the same unsociable hours, still sourcing from the same handful of farms we've trusted from the start. Some things shouldn't be optimised.
