2. What Fileo is

Fileo is a temporary file sharing service. You upload a file, get a short link, and share it. Files are automatically deleted when their expiry time passes. We are not a cloud storage provider — files are not intended to be kept permanently.

3. Your files

You retain full ownership of any files you upload. By uploading, you grant Fileo a limited license to store and serve the file solely for the purpose of delivering it to your recipients. We do not access, read, or share your files except as required to operate the service.

4. Prohibited content

You may not upload files that contain malware, viruses, or any malicious code; illegal content of any kind; content that infringes on third-party intellectual property rights; or content that violates any applicable law. We reserve the right to remove any file and terminate any account at our discretion.

5. Free plan limits

Free accounts may upload files up to 500 MB each, hold a maximum of 3 active links at once, and use up to 500 MB of total storage. Links expire after 1 hour or 24 hours. These limits may change over time.

6. Pro plan

Pro accounts receive increased limits including up to 10 GB per file, up to 10 active links, 3 GB weekly storage, extended expiry options, and access to advanced features such as password protection, download limits, access control, and malware scanning. Pro is currently in testing mode — no payment is required during this period.

7. File deletion

Files are deleted automatically when their expiry time passes. We do not guarantee any minimum retention period beyond what is shown at upload time. We may delete files earlier if they are found to violate these terms.

Fileo is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. We are not liable for lost files, failed uploads, or any damages arising from your use of the service. Use it for non-critical transfers — always keep copies of important files.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. When you upload a file, we store the file itself, its name, size, expiry time, and any access settings you configure. We do not collect payment information during the current testing period.

2. How we use your data

Your email is used solely to identify your account and deliver files you have uploaded. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. File storage

Files are stored on our servers only for the duration of the expiry period you select at upload time. Once a file expires it is permanently deleted. You may also delete files manually from your account at any time.

4. Cookies & local storage

We use browser local storage to remember your session and Pro plan status. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising trackers.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, but no service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Fileo after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
