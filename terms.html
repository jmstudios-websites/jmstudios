Terms & Conditions.

These terms outline how JM Studios handles website projects, launch support, hosting work, and ongoing care.

1. Services

JM Studios provides website design, website builds, managed launch support, domain and DNS guidance, SSL setup, hosting support, and small website updates. The exact services for each project are based on the quote, written messages, or agreed scope.

2. Quotes and project scope

Quotes are based on the information provided before work begins. If the project grows, changes direction, needs extra pages, or requires new functionality, the price and timeline may change before that extra work is completed.

3. Client responsibilities

Clients are responsible for providing accurate business details, content, images, approvals, account access, domain access, and any other materials needed to complete the project. Delays in providing these items may delay the project.

4. Payments and third-party costs

Project payments, hosting fees, domain costs, software subscriptions, and other third-party charges are separate unless clearly included in writing. Third-party services may change their prices, terms, or availability.

5. Hosting, domains, and DNS

JM Studios can help connect domains, configure DNS, activate SSL, and manage hosting. Domain registrars, DNS providers, and hosting platforms are external services, so some propagation delays, outages, or provider-side limits may be outside JM Studios control.

6. Revisions and changes

Reasonable revisions are part of the build process when they fit the agreed direction. Major layout changes, new pages, new features, copy rewrites, or repeated changes after approval may require additional time or cost.

7. Launch and approval

Before launch, clients should review the website for content accuracy, links, spelling, and business details. Once approved and launched, future edits can be handled as updates or through an ongoing care arrangement.

8. Portfolio use

JM Studios may show completed work in a portfolio, case study, or example section unless the client asks in writing for the project to stay private.

9. No guaranteed results

JM Studios builds professional websites and handles practical launch setup, but does not guarantee specific sales, search rankings, traffic, leads, or business outcomes.

10. Limitation of liability

JM Studios is not responsible for indirect losses, third-party service failures, lost files provided by external platforms, or issues caused by changes made outside JM Studios work. Clients should keep copies of important content, images, and account information.

11. Updates to these terms

These terms may be updated as services change. The latest version posted on this page is the version that applies going forward.

12. Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent through the contact form on the JM Studios website.
